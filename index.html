AMEB Connect

A web application for online enrolments for the Australian Music Education Board NSW.

The enrolment process was stressful for the users, the users are typically older and less computer literate.

My aim was to make this process more relaxing by adding a calming colour pallette, making the interaction easier by making a clear definition between the controls and the results

I improved the look and feel of this site adding ameb branding and responsive design.

My role was, improve visual design to existing layout. Implementation of improved design
